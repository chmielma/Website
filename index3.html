ERC St-G UNREST

Understanding the Seismogenic zone of the Hellenic Subduction.


The UNREST project investigates the mechanical behavior of the plate interface in the Hellenic subduction zone. Using advanced seismic imaging and monitoring, we aim to bridge the gap between long-term tectonic deformation and short-term seismic activity.
